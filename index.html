About

What is CSIntel?

I'm Sean — a UK-based builder obsessed with what modern AI makes possible. CSIntel started as a personal lab and turned into a place where ideas actually get shipped.

From recipe tools and social apps to custom Claude agents and workflow automation — everything here is built, tested, and documented. Part showcase, part ongoing experiment in what AI can do.

The goal isn't perfection. It's to build real things, learn by doing, and share what works.

Process

How I build

Three phases I run through on every project.

01

Identify

Find a real problem or an interesting use case — something worth solving, or something that reveals what AI can actually do when pushed.

02

Build

Use Claude and the best available tools to ship fast. Iterate in the open. Don't wait for perfect — get something working and learn from it.

03

Document

Share what works, what didn't, and what's next. The build log matters as much as the thing itself — this is how others (and future me) learn from it.
