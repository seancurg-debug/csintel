Our Story

More Than Just a Café

"This café is more than just a business — it's my passion, my purpose, and a way to honour my mum's life."

Hi, I'm Chandelle Jones — a proud mum of five, with NVQ Level 2 and 3 qualifications and years of experience working my way up in the hospitality industry through determination and resilience.

Carlina's is named in memory of my mum, and everything we do here is driven by her spirit. Whether it's a proper cooked breakfast, a fresh lunch plate, or simply a good cup of coffee — every dish is made with the same care you'd get at home.
