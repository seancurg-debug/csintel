Our Story

More than a café — it's a calling

"This café is more than just a business — it's my passion, my purpose, and a way to honour my mum's life."

Founded by Chandelle Jones — a proud mother of five with NVQ Level 2 & 3 qualifications — Carlina's Café was built on love, loss, and a relentless drive to create something meaningful. Every dish is homemade with care, using locally sourced ingredients.

👩‍🍳
Chandelle Jones
Founder & Head Chef · NVQ Level 2 & 3
